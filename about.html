About CTCONTECH

Small Team. Big Results.

We're a Cape Town web design studio that helps small businesses look professional online — without the agency price tag or the runaround.

Built for Small Business

CTCONTECH started with a simple idea: small businesses deserve great websites too. Not bloated WordPress themes. Not cookie-cutter templates. Real, custom-built sites that perform.

We use the same modern technologies that power enterprise platforms — React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS — but we package them at a price point that makes sense for growing businesses.

When you work with us, you talk directly to the person writing the code. No account managers, no handoffs, no surprises. Just clear communication and fast delivery.
