Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can contact us about your data.

Who we are

CTCONTECH is a practical division of CTCON (Pty) Ltd, providing remote IT support and website development services.

What information we collect

  • Contact details you provide (name, phone number, email address).
  • Enquiry details (your message, preferred contact time, and the issue you need help with).
  • Support-related details you share to troubleshoot (only what’s necessary for the job).
  • Basic technical logs from hosting providers (e.g., IP address, browser type) for security and performance.

How we collect it

  • When you contact us via WhatsApp, email, phone, or through website links that open those services.
  • From basic hosting logs generated when you load pages (typical website operation).

WhatsApp form on the Home page

The “Describe your issue” form on the Home page creates a pre-filled WhatsApp message on your device. Your message is not stored on the website itself.

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotes.
  • To deliver IT support or website services you request.
  • To schedule call-backs and track service delivery when needed.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when needed to deliver the service (for example, with hosting/email providers or platforms you request support for).

Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for service delivery, support history, and reasonable business record-keeping.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate for a small business service provider. No system is perfectly secure, but we aim to protect your data from unauthorised access.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information where applicable.
