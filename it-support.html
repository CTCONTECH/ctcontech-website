Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you respond to an emergency?
▼
Response time depends on your plan: Essentials responds within 24 hours, Professional within 4 hours, Premium within 2 hours during business hours. Premium plan subscribers also get 24/7 monitoring for critical issues.
Can you access my files remotely?
▼
Yes, with your permission. We use industry-standard remote access tools (TeamViewer, AnyDesk) so you can see exactly what we're doing. You maintain control—you can close the connection anytime.
What if you can't fix it remotely?
▼
We'll diagnose what's wrong and explain your options. If hardware replacement or on-site setup is needed, we'll give you an upfront quote before proceeding. No surprises.
Do you offer backup and disaster recovery?
▼
Absolutely. We set up automated cloud backup, test recovery regularly, and keep offline copies. It's part of responsible IT management, and we make it simple.
Are there contracts or lock-ins?
▼
No. All plans are month-to-month—cancel anytime. We want to earn your trust every month, not trap you in a contract.
What areas do you service?
▼
Remote support available nationwide across South Africa and internationally. All support is delivered remotely via industry-standard tools like TeamViewer and AnyDesk, so your location doesn't matter.
What if you can't fix the issue?
▼
We'll clearly explain the problem and your options. If it requires hardware replacement, vendor support, or specialist intervention, we'll let you know upfront with recommendations and optionally help coordinate the next steps.
How often should backups be tested?
▼
We recommend monthly restore tests. Our maintenance plans include regular verification to ensure backups are actually recoverable. A backup that can't be restored isn't a backup.
Can you manage multiple locations or offices?
▼
Yes. We support multi-site setups remotely. If on-site visits are needed, we handle setup and training in the Cape Town area and can coordinate with local partners elsewhere.
Is there a price for emergency out-of-hours support?
▼
Premium plan includes 24/7 monitoring. Essentials and Professional plans support business hours only. For out-of-hours work in Essentials or Professional plans, additional charges apply. Chat with us to discuss your specific after-hours needs.

Our Scope: We provide practical IT support and operational assistance. Formal cybersecurity audits, compliance assessments, and penetration testing are outside our scope.
